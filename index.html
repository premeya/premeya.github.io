Our Approach

Requirements

For every client need we try and understand what is the objective behind that need. A Deep understanding of the need and client's business would enable us to define the path towards the acheiving the objective. While we understand that the final product vision could change during the iterations, by understanding the client's objective and business, we tend to keep these changes to the minimum by anticipating the changes. We do this using wireframes, small prototypes and enough documentation.

Design

“Two of the most important characteristics of good design are dis- coverability and understanding.” — Don Norman

While designing a solution, we try to look at how a person using the product would discover and understand in a way that would seem natural and unambigious.
